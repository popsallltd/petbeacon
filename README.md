## How It Works

**For pet owners:** Register your pet and microchip number. If your pet ever goes missing and someone finds it, they use the app to locate a nearby helper with a scanner. **For finders:** Open the app, see helpers near you on a map, walk the pet over, get it scanned. The owner is notified automatically. If no helper is available or the chip won't read, post a Found Pet Alert with a photo that goes out to nearby users.
